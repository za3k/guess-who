Your secret person

  • You and your opponent both load this webpage. Don't look at each other's screens.
  • Take turns asking yes/no questions, like "Does your character have black hair?"
  • Eliminate characters by clicking them to grey them out. Click them again if you make a mistake, to return them.
  • If you think you know your opponent's character, you can guess instead of asking a question. If you guess right, you win! If you guess wrong, you lose.
Guess Who? was made by Zachary "za3k" Vance as part of Hack-a-Day, a challenge to do one new project each day in November. The source code is available on github. Faces are taken from thispersondoesnotexist.com.
